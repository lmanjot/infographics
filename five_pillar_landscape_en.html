The Cellular Battle for Hair Growth

PRP: The Bio-Stimulator

Platelet-Rich Plasma (PRP) harnesses a high concentration of your own platelets to deliver a powerful cocktail of growth factors directly to the follicle.

1

Growth Factor Release

Activated platelets release growth factors that saturate the follicle environment.

2

DP & Bulge Activation

These factors signal the dermal papilla and stem cells to push dormant follicles into growth phase.

Net Effect: Initiates Growth Cycle

Exosomes: The Messengers

Exosomes are microscopic vesicles rich in growth factors and genetic material. They act as direct communicators, delivering regenerative instructions.

1

Direct Signaling

Exosomes are taken up by dermal papilla and bulge region cells, releasing their cargo.

2

Cell Proliferation

The instructions cause cells to multiply and regenerate the follicle for a new growth phase.

Net Effect: Rejuvenation & Activation

Minoxidil: The Stimulator

Originally a blood pressure medication, minoxidil's primary mechanism is vasodilation, but it also has direct effects on the follicle's growth cycle.

1

Vasodilation

Opens potassium channels in blood vessels, increasing blood flow and nutrient supply to the follicle.

2

Anagen Phase Extension

Directly stimulates DP cells and extends the growth phase, resulting in longer and thicker hair.

Net Effect: Enhanced Blood Flow & Longer Growth

Finasteride: The Protector

Finasteride works systemically to protect the hair follicle from the miniaturizing effects of dihydrotestosterone (DHT), the main culprit in male pattern baldness.

1

Enzyme Inhibition

Blocks the 5-alpha-reductase enzyme, preventing the conversion of testosterone to DHT.

2

Follicle Protection

With less DHT, DP cells no longer receive the signal to shrink. This stops miniaturization.

Net Effect: Stops Miniaturization

Supplements

Supplements work systemically to create an optimal internal environment for healthy hair growth by providing essential building blocks and protection.

1

Hormonal Balance

Saw palmetto can help lower DHT levels, while vitamins and minerals nourish the follicle.

2

Cellular Protection

Antioxidants like curcumin and vitamin E protect the follicle from oxidative stress.

Net Effect: Provides Building Blocks & Reduces Damage
